About

Engineer by craft,
generalist by nature.

I started my career in Costa Rica in 2007 and have spent the last nine years in Germany, where I grew from mid-level engineer to senior at samedi GmbH — a healthcare SaaS company. I was a core contributor to their Patient Portal product: a white-label, multi-tenant platform deployed across hospitals and clinics across Europe.

Before samedi, I built UIs at Backcountry.com — one of the largest outdoor e-commerce platforms in the US — and started my career at Grupo Nación in Costa Rica, one of Latin America's leading media groups.

I work across the full stack but I'm particularly strong in the Rails + React + TypeScript combination. I care deeply about clean architecture, performance, and making codebases that future engineers will thank you for.
